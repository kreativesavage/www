Don't trip

The $160 you never have to pay

Miss the Wyoming annual report and the LLC gets dissolved — bringing it back costs $60 late report + $100 penalty = $160. It's due the first day of your formation-anniversary month. File on time and this line stays at zero. Treat that date as sacred.

Annual report

File it yourself for $60. A service can charge up to $299 for the same 5-minute filing.

Form 5472 + 1120

A DIY fax costs cents. Paid prep of a zero-activity return runs into the hundreds.

Registered agent

Worth paying — ~$149/yr is fair, and you legally can't keep the LLC without one.

03 / STEPS

The route,
tag by tag

No US visit. No US visa. No SSN. Nine moves, in order — the email is the anchor everything hangs off.

1

Register a domain ~$12/yr

Grab your brand's domain (Namecheap, Cloudflare, Google). It's the ground your email and store both stand on.

2

Set up a pro email $16.80/mo

Google Workspace on your domain — you@yourbrand.com. This is the login and contact identity for the LLC, the bank, the store, and the IRS. Keep it business-only so official mail never gets lost.

3

Form the US LLC $599 one-time

Use a formation service (bundle covers the state filing, registered agent year 1, and the EIN — no SSN needed) or file direct with the state to save. Wyoming is a common pick for non-residents.

4

Lock in your EIN bundled

Keep the CP 575 letter (and a 147C if you request one) forever — banks ask for it. The EIN belongs to the entity for life, even through a lapse and reinstatement.

5

Open a US bank account $0

Mercury, using the EIN + formation docs + 147C. Enter your real operating country as principal place of business, not the agent address — that mismatch is what triggers a decline.

6

Build the store $348/yr

Shopify Basic on your domain, with Printify for print-on-demand fulfillment. Add products, write your five legal policies, connect it to the bank.

7

Add mailbox + phone $99 + $99/yr

A stable US mailbox so IRS letters reach you (keep the exact box number consistent with your records), and a US phone number to clear Shopify's verification.

8

Turn on the payment rails fees only

Primary: Shopify Payments → Mercury. Backup: PayPal Business (basic mailbox may fail its address check). Home: Mercury wires to your local bank; a bridge like Payoneer/Wise if a channel needs it. Watch double-conversion costs.

9

Stay compliant, every year $60 + $149

Annual report (on time, $60). Registered agent renewal (~$149). Form 5472 + pro forma 1120 by fax, due April 15 — required even at zero income. In year one, formation costs you paid count as reportable contributions to capital.
